My Travel Bucket List

United States

Average Expenses: $2500 - $4000

Specialities: Grand Canyon, Times Square, Statue of Liberty, Silicon Valley.

The USA offers diverse experiences from coast to coast, featuring world-class cities, natural wonders, and cultural landmarks. Whether it's exploring national parks or enjoying Hollywood, it's a dream destination for many.

Japan

Average Expenses: $2000 - $3000

Specialities: Mount Fuji, Kyoto Temples, Cherry Blossoms, Anime Culture.

Japan is a blend of tradition and innovation. From its tranquil shrines and temples to futuristic cities and tech, Japan offers an unforgettable cultural immersion, rich in history, nature, and food.

Paris, France

Average Expenses: $1800 - $2500

Specialities: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, French Cuisine, Romantic Ambiance.

Known as the City of Light, Paris is famous for its art, fashion, and culture. Visitors enjoy romantic strolls, historic architecture, and world-renowned museums and cafes.

Bangalore, India

Average Expenses: $800 - $1500

Specialities: Tech Hub, Botanical Gardens, Street Food, Pleasant Climate.

Often called the Silicon Valley of India, Bangalore is a vibrant city known for its modern infrastructure, booming tech industry, and dynamic culture blending tradition and innovation.
